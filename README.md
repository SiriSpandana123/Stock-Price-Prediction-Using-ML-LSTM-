# Stock-Price-Prediction-Using-ML-LSTM-
LSTM networks excel at capturing sequential market patterns, and when combined with sentiment analysis, they outperform traditional models, making them a powerful tool for accurate stock price prediction and financial decision-making.
Predicting stock prices with high accuracy is a critical objective in financial analytics, given its significant impact on investment strategies and market forecasting. This paper explores the efficacy of Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks, a type of recurrent neural network, in forecasting stock prices. 
LSTM networks are particularly adept at modeling sequential data due to their ability to capture long-term dependencies and temporal patterns. We apply an LSTM-based approach to a comprehensive dataset of historical stock prices, trading volumes, and financial indicators. Our methodology involves rigorous preprocessing, feature engineering, and hyperparameter tuning to optimize model performance. 
Comparative analysis demonstrates that LSTM models substantially outperform traditional statistical methods and other machine learning algorithms, such as linear regression and support vector machines, in terms of prediction accuracy and reliability. The study further investigates the integration of additional features like sentiment analysis from financial news and social media to enhance model performance. The results highlight LSTM networks as a powerful tool for stock price prediction, offering valuable insights for both investors and financial analysts seeking to leverage advanced machine learning techniques in financial decision-making.
This abstract emphasizes the strengths of LSTM networks in the context of stock price prediction and includes aspects such as feature integration and comparative analysis, making it suitable for a detailed research paper.
